UEP BALANGAW attends KATADUNGAN 2025- Leadership in Motion: From Principles to Practice

By UEP BALANGAW on November 04, 2025

The University Student Council (USC) of the University of Eastern Philippines proudly concluded Katungdanan 2025 Leadership in Motion: From Principles to Practice on October 25 - 26, 2025, held at the UEP White Beach Pavilion, Catarman, Northern Samar.


The event served as a vital platform for nurturing the next generation of student leaders.The training was an uplifting blend of insightful sessions and collaborative activities, leaving student leaders with excitement and armed with practical knowledge.


UEP Balangaw is proud that members had the distinct privilege of participating in this enriching leadership training. We are confident that the knowledge, skills, and connections gained will significantly contribute to our organization's mission and impact. We eagerly anticipate applying these lessons within our community, fostering a culture of leadership, collaboration, and innovation.


Inspired with Robin Sharma's words, "Leadership is not about a title or a designation, It's about impact, influence, and inspiration".
