INTERNAL COMMITTEE

Focuses on members' welfare, engagement, and personal development to foster a supportive and collaborative environment within the organization. It also supervises the Secretariat Committee, which handles documentation, attendance tracking, and other administrative responsibilities.

EXTERNAL COMMITTEE

Builds partnerships and manages communication with other organizations, institutions, and networks. It also oversees logistics and other requirements needed to ensure activities run smoothly.

FINANCE COMMITTEE

Manages the organization's financial operations, including budgeting, fundraising, and resource allocation to sustain its programs and activities.

MULTIMEDIA COMMITTEE

Handles promotions by producing creative materials such as publications, social media content, and other visual or digital outputs.

DOCUMENTATION COMMITTEE

Documents and archives the organization's activities and milestones through photos, videos, and written reports to preserve its history and promote transparency.
